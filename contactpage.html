    What is your return policy?

We accept returns for unused and unworn items within 30 days of receipt. To start a return:

  • Create a Return: Log into your account and select the items to return.
  • Pack the Items: Use the original packaging or a secure alternative.
  • Ship It Back: Use the prepaid return label provided.

    • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the return.

      Can I purchase on approval?

    Yes! You can try items at home before making a final decision. Return unworn items within 30 days if they’re not the right fit or style for you. Be sure to keep tags attached and items in their original condition to qualify for a full refund.

      How can I file a claim or complaint?

    We’re sorry to hear you’ve had an issue! If you received a damaged item, the wrong size, or have any concerns about your order, you can easily file a claim or complaint

  • Contact Us: Provide your order number and a brief description of the issue.
  • Photos: If applicable, attach pictures of the problem (e.g., damaged or incorrect item).
  • Process: Our team will review your case and respond within 1-3 business days with a solution, such as a replacement, refund, or exchange.

    • We’re committed to ensuring your satisfaction and will resolve your issue as quickly as possible!
